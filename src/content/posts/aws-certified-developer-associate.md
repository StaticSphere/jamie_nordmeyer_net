And that’s two down! Last September (2022), I received my AWS Certified Solutions Architect - Associate certification. This last Wednesday (5/10/2023), I received my AWS Certified Developer - Associate certification. Humorously, I thought this exam was actually harder than the Solutions Architect exam, yet I scored higher on this one than the first. I’m guessing that’s because I already had AWS exam experience going into the Developer exam and knew what to expect, whereas the Solutions Architect exam was the first AWS exam I had ever taken. Either way, it’s out of the way now. 🙂

I’m not planning to pursue additional certifications right now, so I have a good two years before I need to worry about studying again. Woot!
